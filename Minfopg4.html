Simple Home Alarm System Project

For this project, I worked with a group to build a simple home alarm system using the Arduino Mega 2560. My main role was hardware specialist(responsible for selecting, testing, and integrating the alarm system's hardware components). In addition to aiding with research and planning, I provided a hardware description, System Block Diagram, and parts list/Bill of Materials(BOM) for the project. After that, we created a PowerPoint presentation that explained the purpose of our system/how it works and recorded an oral presentation.
