About Me

I am based in the Hampton Roads area of Virginia and have graduated from ECPI University with a Bachelors of Science in Electronic Systems Engineering Technology with a concentration in Mechatronics.

I have always found projects that involve computers/electronics/coding very interesting and exiting ever since took my first technology-based class in middle school. Where I was first introduced to CNC machining, 2D/3D modeling, woodworking, robotics, coding, and much more. Today, I am thrilled to continue being on the path to gaining a better understanding of electronic systems throughout the ever-evolving world of technology.

Skills and experience

Ability to design and configure computer, communication, and control systems; analyze typical circuits used in communication systems; create 2D and 3D designs for engineering parts using CAD software; implement hydraulic and pneumatic systems; and analyze forces and their effects on systems, install routers/modems, troubleshoot wireless networks. Also has significant experience dealing with typical workstation hardware and software (including assembling, maintaining, and troubleshooting computer hardware and software).

Experience with using Autodesk Inventor/Maya/AutoCAD, SolidWorks, MathCAD, NI Multisim, LabVIEW, PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers), Microcontrollers, Microsoft Office Suite (Word / Excel / PowerPoint /Outlook), HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and Python.
